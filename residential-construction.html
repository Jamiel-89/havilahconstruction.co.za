House Building

House building encompasses the complete construction of residential properties, from the foundation to the final finishing touches. Havilah Construction ensures high-quality workmanship at every stage, including:

  • Design and Planning: Collaborating with clients to develop tailored architectural designs and layouts.
  • Foundation and Framing: Constructing sturdy and durable foundations and structural frameworks.
  • Electrical and Plumbing Work: Installing efficient and safe systems to meet modern living standards.
  • Finishing Touches: Applying final details, such as paint, fixtures, and trims, to deliver a ready-to-live-in home.

Flooring

Flooring is a crucial element of a home's functionality and aesthetic appeal. Havilah Construction offers a variety of flooring solutions, including:

  • Material Selection: Helping clients choose from options like tiles, hardwood, laminate, vinyl, or carpet to match their style and budget.
  • Professional Installation: Ensuring proper leveling and seamless installation for durability and visual appeal.
  • Custom Designs: Providing decorative patterns and unique finishes to elevate interior design.

Roofing

Roofing is essential for protecting homes against weather elements while adding to the overall aesthetic. Havilah Construction specializes in:

  • Roof Design: Tailoring roofing styles, such as gable, hip, or flat roofs, to suit the client's preferences and structural requirements.
  • Material Options: Offering choices like asphalt shingles, metal, tile, or thatch to meet durability and aesthetic needs.
  • Installation and Repair: Delivering efficient roofing installations and maintenance services to ensure longevity.

Plastering

Plastering enhances both the interior and exterior walls, providing smooth, durable surfaces for painting or decoration. Havilah Construction excels in:

  • Surface Preparation: Cleaning and leveling walls to ensure proper plaster application.
  • Application Techniques: Using skilled techniques to apply plaster evenly, whether for plain or decorative finishes.
  • Durability Assurance: Ensuring the plaster is resistant to cracking and suitable for various weather conditions.

We deliver our Services with a commitment to quality, timeliness, and client satisfaction, ensuring that every residential construction project by Havilah Construction meets the highest standards.
